Relatia – CRM Web Design

Relatia is a CRM SaaS platform designed to help businesses grow, engage, and retain customers through a unified dashboard.

relatia.com

About the Project

Relatia is a modern all-in-one Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform built for small and medium-sized sales teams to streamline operations—from acquiring leads to retaining loyal customers.

Design Goal

Modern
Monochromatic
Intuitive
Conversion Focused

Challenges

Scattered Sales Process

Sales reps used disjointed tools, leading to inefficient workflows and data silos.

Poor User Onboarding

New users struggled to understand the system due to lack of visual clarity and guidance.

Low Lead Conversion

Minimal insights and friction-heavy UI reduced lead-to-customer conversion rate.

User Research

Overwhelming dashboards Generic, outdated UI
Pain Points Identified
Confusing navigation Lack of customization

Design Process

Discovery

Studied CRMs like Pipedrive, HubSpot, Zoho

Wireframing

Mapped out flows: user onboarding → deal management → goal tracking

Design System

Built a shared token-based system for colors, spacing, and interaction states

UI Design

Prioritized visual hierarchy, card-based layouts, and modular components
